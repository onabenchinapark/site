This is my open journal of awakening.

The purpose of my work will always be this. Just living. Being, becoming, and expressing all that is.

That is this body of work. And so here are some ways this will look:

- contemplations on living, awakening, the mind, and more

- artistic writings on the human condition, love, suffering, and what it means to be

- practical meditations to follow for anyone on their path

- explorations and research into the nature of consciousness and reality

- applications of contemplative insight, focused on transdisciplinary approaches of reducing suffering and increasing wellbeing

Here is the grander vision:

- a knowledge base of enlightenment; how can we effectively share our best wisdom, practices, experiences, and more at large?

- a meta-entity of collective wisdom; how can we scale trust and meaning and coordination in the right ways?
